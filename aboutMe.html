About me

I'll be honest with all of you, I don't think I am the most interesting person nor I have an enourmous knowledge about any of the stuff I'll write about. I am just a guy who really loves coding with all his heart and who accidently got really into speciality coffee.

Does this fact make the blog more or less interesting? Personally, I don't think so. I've always prefered stories about strangers just like me. When I read a book I want to be able to relate myself to the main character. If they become to distant the reading is less fun. "No Longer Human" by Osamu Dazai? Very interesting concept but I am not a psychopath. "Dog Songs" by Mary Oliver? Yeah that's more like it, even if I am not a dog.

That how I also feel about everything I do. I want things to be approachable and not elitists. Speciality coffee is delicious, but if you prefer instant we can still have a civilized conversation. If you think that coding is only for smart, academic people then we have a problem. I code everyday and I want to show what and how I do it. If you can relate, keep on reading!
